JASPER’S WORLD — AI PROMPT CONTRACT GUARDRAIL
(Preventing AI Overreach & Improvisation)

PURPOSE
-------
This guardrail defines a REQUIRED CONTRACT whenever changes are delegated to an AI.
Its purpose is to prevent the AI from "helpfully" modifying systems that were not
explicitly requested.

================================================================
REQUIRED PROMPT STRUCTURE
================================================================

Every AI task MUST define:

1. INTENT
2. ALLOWED AXES
3. FORBIDDEN AXES
4. SUCCESS CONDITION

================================================================
EXAMPLE (VALID)
================================================================

Intent:
- Restore background depth

Allowed:
- Scale
- Parallax

Forbidden:
- Camera
- Z order
- Gameplay contrast

Success:
- Background layers feel separated without harming readability

================================================================
INVALID PROMPTS
================================================================

- "Make it look better"
- "Fix the scene"
- Any prompt without forbidden axes

================================================================
ENFORCEMENT RULE
================================================================

If an AI modifies a forbidden axis:
→ Output is INVALID regardless of quality

================================================================
FINAL RULE
================================================================

The AI does not choose scope.
YOU do.

END OF AI PROMPT CONTRACT GUARDRAIL
